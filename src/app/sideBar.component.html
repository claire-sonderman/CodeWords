Instructions

Overview:

This game involves word deduction. Players form 2 teams (red and blue) with one Leader on each team. Each Team Leader has 9 (red) or 8 (blue) code words in the display that their teammates need to guess. Team Leaders can give their teammates one word clues.

 

Turns:

The Team Leader gives a one word clue and a number. The number is how many code words in the display that the word describes or is related to. Teammates can guess that many words on the board. The turn is over if a Neutral card or the opponent’s words are guessed. If the Death word is guessed, then that team loses the game.

 

Win:

The game ends when one team's code words are all guessed (flipped). That team wins.
